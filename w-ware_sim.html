Worm Malware Explained

A worm is a type of malware that can rapidly spread and cause severe disruption once installed on a computer or network.

Worms are capable of copying themselves into other programs and systems automatically, often without the user realizing it.

They may overload servers, consume large amounts of system resources, spread across networks, and even crash computers or services.

Worms are commonly spread through phishing emails, infected downloads, malicious advertisements, or unsafe websites.

Always be cautious before clicking suspicious links or downloading unknown files from the internet.
